Game story
How it began

In a time when the boundaries of reality were stretched by cosmic mishaps, a glitch in the time-space continuum merged disparate eras and beings onto an intriguing haven known as UniDelta. This semi-flat asteroid houses an amalgamation of individuals, relics, and cultures, spanning from ancient Egypt to the modern age.



Clash of Ambitions

At the heart of this tale stand two figures: Bastet, embodying the essence of heroism, and Apep, the embodiment of villainy. Their ambitions clash, echoing throughout UniDelta. Amidst these forces, individuals from various epochs find themselves entwined, some aspiring for peace and growth, while others harbor ambitions beyond mere warfare.
In the face of mounting tensions, the Bastet army emerges as a defense mechanism, a guardian for the coexisting population. However, their strength requires collective empowerment. The inhabitants face a pivotal decision, uniting to determine how best to support the Bastet army, enabling their valiant efforts against adversity.
